About Us

Students of SFHS

They participate in a variety of extracurricular activities, such as sports teams, student government, clubs, community service organizations, or academic competitions. These activities help them develop leadership skills, teamwork, and a well-rounded perspective.

They take on leadership roles within their school and community, showing initiative, responsibility, and the ability to inspire others. They may serve as captains of sports teams, presidents of clubs, or organizers of community events.

They are actively involved in their communities, volunteering their time for various causes or organizations. Whether it's through volunteering at local shelters, tutoring younger students, or participating in environmental initiatives, they demonstrate a commitment to making a positive impact.
